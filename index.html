About Taskzo

We Don't Just Connect — We Take Responsibility

Taskzo is a verified local service platform built for real India. We connect customers with background-checked, skilled professionals — and we stay accountable for the quality of every service.

Unlike other platforms that simply list numbers or assign random workers, Taskzo carefully onboards every provider, monitors their performance through real customer feedback, and removes anyone who doesn't meet our quality standards.

  • ⭐After every service, we collect genuine customer feedback — no fake reviews

  • 🎯You choose your expert — no forced assignment, full transparency
